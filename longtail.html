꼬리가 길면 정말 밟힐까요? 밟으셨나요?
There is a Korean saying, “If a tail is long, it will be stepped on.” It means that no matter how secretly you commit a wrongdoing, if you repeat it over time, you will eventually be exposed. This work takes the proverb literally and conducts a straightforward experiment to see whether a long tail can, in fact, be stepped on.
